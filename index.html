This game is created and inspired from the classic Lucky 9 Card Game but with an easier and quick step to win. No bet and bankers, just get the first 10 points by winning every round to beat the bot and win the game.

HOW TO PLAY

  • Draw your first 2 cards, it is randomly distributed.
  • If you luckily get a total of nine from your 2 cards, for example, you got 2 of spades and 7 of hearts which means you got Lucky 9! Automatically you win the round and get 1 point.
  • If your 2 cards total a two-digit number, the last digit counts as the value of the hand. For example:
    8 + 4 = 14 which counts as 4.
    Take note that cards A, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 7, 8,and 9 are all counted according to their numerical value. Cards 10 have novalue.
  • If you don't get lucky 9 you can either make a "hit" or draw a third card. This where your strategy comes to play.
  • Third draw can become much less favorable after a "hit". For example, if your first two cards have a total of 4 and the third card you draw is the 8 of diamonds, your hand total becomes 12 which counts as 2.
  • To win the round you must get Lucky 9 or your total card is much higher than the bot total card.
  • Another round will be played until you or the bot get the first 10 points and be the lucky winner.
